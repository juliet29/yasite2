Partnering with the Youth Alliance for Democratic Change .

The concept of Youth Alliance for Peaceful Democratic Change is as a result of in-depth study of the activities of the youths during election periods. The study has shown the devastating effect of thuggery during this period. The study showed that majority act of thuggery emanate from influences from the tertiary intuitions, which breed cultists of various destructive tendencies.

The Organization in that respect has invested more on this leadership seminar annually, to encourage our youths to embrace peace and dignity in pursuit of any goal. The YADPC equally embark on advocacy visit to various Institution educate them on the need to propagate this agenda to their various communities and parents that no electoral position is worth the blood of any individual.

A recent survey conducted by YADPC showed that politicians use things like propaganda, ethnic and religious sentiments to lure the innocent and unsuspecting youths into thuggery and violence for their selfish political and socio-economic goals. The Youth Alliance for Peaceful Democratic Change (YAPDC) wants to address this issue before the 2015 elections through procurement of air space in Dream FM Enugu.

We hope to use this platform to facilitate the following YADPC objectives:

  • To educate the youths on the dangers of political thuggery.
  • To engage the youths in positive political activism as a way of reducing idleness and restiveness amongst them.
  • To encourage them to believe in themselves.
  • To support those who have interest in politics as a way of reducing unemployment.
  • To carry the campaign of peaceful democratic change, accountability in leadership and in political governance to the campuses and rural areas through seminars, workshops, etc.
  • To provide a platform for members of JCIHP/YAPDC to launch into active politics and support for other to persons or organizations with similar objectives.

The Organization in that respect has invested more on this leadership seminar annually, to encourage our youths to embrace peace and dignity in pursuit of any goal. The YADPC equally embark on advocacy visit to various Institution educate them on the need to propagate this agenda to their various communities and parents that no electoral position is worth the blood of any individual.

The YAPDC held a rally under the platform of PDP Fan Club at the burial ceremony of the mother of Chairman of PDP, Enugu State Engr. Vita Abba on 11 October, 2013. The YAPDC equally participated at the rally at Okpara Sqaure on 16th of October, 2013 for the official flag-off of PDP LG Election Campaign in Enugu State.

The YADPC also sent two of its members each to all the LGs to monitor the just concluded LG Election held on 2 November, 2013. The essence is to ascertain the turnout of electorates for election and if they believe in the system.

The total number of participants in attendance is 89 which includes; schools’ executive and representatives from School of Dental Technology Trans Ekulu, Enugu, Institute of Management and Technology (IMT)Enugu, Enugu State University of Science and Technology, (ESUTECT), Enugu to mention just a few.

Partnering with Team 2018 .

Team 2015 is an initiative of Women’s International League for Peace & Freedom Nigeria (WILPF) that focuses on creating awareness on political participation as a means of exercising civic and political rights of women, educating on women’s human rights, mobilizing a strong women’s constituency and supporting women aspiring for political positions come 2015 in Nigeria. The JCIHP has signed an MOU with WILPF to respond to challenges faced by women in Nigeria in the area of governance and democratic processes in the nation.

This has yielded results as some of the women projected by the Organization were made Deputy Chairmen in the just concluded Local Government Election in Enugu State. An example is the Deputy-Chairman Elect of Udi Local Government Area Mrs. Uche Eze who was sponsored by JCIHP.
