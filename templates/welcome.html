Learn More About Us

The Youth Accountability Forum is a platform where the youths (both male and female) are trained and mentored on how to implement the principles of Open Government and hold their government accountable.

Through engaging young people in accountability, there is an opportunity to connect power to youth and youth to power, redefining the relationship between duty bearers and young people.

Working with young people always brings great joy, but it gives us a greater sense of satisfaction when we can empower them to get into spaces where they can actually interact and engage with decision-makers. We believe that youth-led accountability for the Open Government Partnership is important because it will allow young people to be meaningfully engaged in this new development framework.
